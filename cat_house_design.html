Top 6 DIY Cat House Designs for Outdoor and Indoor


These 30 unique DIY cat house ideas for outdoor and indoor use are a smart way to snuggle up your little furry friend and help them unwind after a full day of eating and playing. These lovely and simple indoor and outdoor cat home ideas are reasonably priced, and you'll have a lot of fun building them. So use your imagination to create a location for your cats to rest, play, eat, and so on. Furthermore, they are a fantastic project that you can easily complete at home instead of purchasing pricey cat houses from the store.The most important and critical step is to choose the right material for your cats, whether you're building an indoor or outdoor cat house. Take some time for your cats and give them these really convenient, clever, and comfortable new homes!



  1. Best DIY Little Cat House

For all the cat lovers out there, here is a two-in-one cat housing and side table that is a very attractive piece of furniture. This DIY project is not only beautiful, but it also serves as a terrific stress reliever. You'll also need plywood, pocket hole screws, beacon glue, grit sandpaper, hole saw tack cloth, a drill, table saw, and a finish nailer, among other things.


  1. Cat House Tepee From An Old TV Tray

A cat home made out of a tepee? Making this sounds like a lot of fun! It is quite handy and convenient for cats, and it can be quickly built with certain items that are lying around the house. It may be customised to fit your cat's size, and it also serves as a terrific house decoration when placed in a corner of a living room. To accomplish this project, you'll also need a dowel, TV tray, screws, drill, stain, fabric, thread, hot glue, plywood, dowel, jute rope, felt, and some pillow stuffing.


  1. Cardboard Cat Condo

To construct this cardboard DIY cat house for your wonderful little pet, you'll need a corrugated cardboard box, a cutter, a pencil, a compass, a ruler, white glue, and a paintbrush. This house is incredibly cute, and it could become your cat's new favourite place to lounge and rest all of the time. Even if it's just in one corner of the living room, it'll make a wonderful design item to go with the rest of the house's classic wooden furnishings.


  1. Outdoor Waterproof Cat House

To save outdoor cats from getting cold or sick, an ingenious shelter has been designed to help them get through this difficult time. Vehicle tyres were used to construct this shelter. Furthermore, one tyre can easily house two to three cats.


  1. Kitty House From Magnetic Balls

This is a fantastic and entertaining kitty house made of magnetic balls. It's perfect for small cats because they're light and can easily climb up, play, and even sleep in it without damaging it. Furthermore, you'll need a variety of colourful magnets to make this house very brilliant and vibrant.


  1. Homemade Cat Tent

Our cats, like us, require the simplicity and comfort of sleeping calmly in a secure environment. If you have an old t-shirt that is no longer useful, you can use it for this fantastic project. This is a simple DIY cat tent that doesn't require much time or effort. Simply make sure you have an old t-shirt, wire hangers, and a piece of cardboard on hand for your cat's new home.
